Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an event on EventHub?

To create an event, you need to first register or log in to your EventHub account. Then, click on the "Create Event" button on your dashboard and follow the guided steps to set up your event details, ticketing, and promotions.

How can I promote my event on EventHub?

EventHub offers various promotion options including featured listings, email marketing, social media integration, and partnerships with relevant communities. You can explore these options in the "Promote" section after creating your event.

What payment methods are accepted for ticket purchases?

EventHub accepts all major credit cards, PayPal, and in select regions, we also support local payment methods and digital wallets. The available payment options will be displayed during the checkout process.

How can I get a refund for a cancelled event?

If an event is cancelled by the organizer, refunds are typically processed automatically within 5-7 business days. You will receive an email notification about the cancellation and refund process. If you haven't received your refund after this period, please contact our support team.
